Foundation CSS Framework

Foundation is a product of the company ZURB, which calls itself a product design company. Their homepage is at https://zurb.com/.

Dependencies: requires jQuery
Online documentation: https://get.foundation/sites/docs/
Forum: https://foundation.discourse.group/

Offers several plugins, which are installed separately. The plug-ins use data attributes, like "data-accordion" and "data-tooltip". Many plugins are configurable.
