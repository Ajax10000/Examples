Today when applying for a web developer position it is not uncommon for the applicant to be asked to take two tests before a face-to-face interview. The first is an online quiz that tests your knowledge of the subject matter. The second is a coding test where you are asked to write or fix a short program.

Given that, it is appropriate to consider testing your JavaScript skills. There are various free online JavaScript quizzes available online. Below you will find some links to some of these free online quizzes.

  • https://www.w3schools.com/js/js_quiz.asp 25 questions, no time limit
  • https://www.javatpoint.com/javascript-quiz Easy tests, but a bit flaky, buggy (especially when it provides the answers). It does provide many tests though.
  • https://www.tutorialspoint.com/javascript/javascript_online_quiz.htm 10 questions
  • http://perfectionkills.com/javascript-quiz/ 14 questions
  • https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/javascript-quiz-set-1/ 10 questions
  • https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/javascript-quiz-set-2/?ref=lbp 10 questions
  • https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/javascript-quiz-set-3/?ref=lbp 10 questions
  • https://www.techbeamers.com/javascript-quiz-for-web-developers/ 25 questions
  • https://www.khanacademy.org/computing/computer-programming/html-css-js/html-css-js-intro/e/quiz--javascript-recap 12 questions
  • https://www.wscubetech.com/quiz-test-javascript 30 questions, 30 minute time limit
  • http://davidshariff.com/js-quiz/ Difficult test!
